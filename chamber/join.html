NP Membership Benefits

No membership fee for nonprofit organizations.

Access to community events, basic training, and nonprofit networking opportunities.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Includes Chamber event access, business networking, and member directory listing.

Good option for small businesses getting started.

Silver Membership Benefits

Includes Bronze benefits plus event discounts, training opportunities, and added advertising options.

Good option for growing businesses that want more visibility.

Gold Membership Benefits

Includes Silver benefits plus priority spotlight placement, premium advertising, and special event recognition.

Best option for businesses wanting the highest visibility.
